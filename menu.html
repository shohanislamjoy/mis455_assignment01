Espresso

A shot of strong black coffee made by forcing steam through ground coffee beans.

Cappuccino

Espresso mixed with steamed milk foam to create a creamy texture.

Latte

Espresso combined with steamed milk for a smooth and creamy coffee.

Americano

Espresso diluted with hot water to create a milder coffee.

Mocha

Espresso with steamed milk and chocolate syrup, topped with whipped cream.

Macchiato

Espresso with a small amount of milk, usually foamed, added.
